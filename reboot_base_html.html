Our mission

Reboot is a website to help those in the DC area access local and online crisis resources that suit their needs. At the time of its creation, there was no other area-specific database addressing multiple categories of issues. This website hopes to fill that vacancy and encourage others to do so as well, one city at a time.

Reboot is run by Girls Who Code, a nonprofit organization.
