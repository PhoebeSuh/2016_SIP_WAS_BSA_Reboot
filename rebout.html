About Reboot

     Reboot is a website created in order help those in the DC area access local and online crisis resources that suit their needs. This site is the only comprehensive list of DC resources for multiple issues, including several local nonprofits and service providers. Reboot also provides a database of programs to help survivors who may not know what steps to take next.     Founded during Girls Who Code 2016 by Phoebe Suh Phoebe, 15, is a dancer, nerd, and twue fwend., Kristen ElvisKristen , 16, is BSA's finest Running Man dancer., Sophie NicholsSophie Nichols is an excellent programmer and an even better poet., and Willitta CooperWillitta has the best blanket in the house., Reboot rose from a desire to help DC residents get the help they often needed. Though Girls Who Code 2016 has ended, Reboot is an ongoing project, and plans to update or expand are on the horizon.
